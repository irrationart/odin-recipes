Macaron

Description

The recipe below should not be used as an actual recipe.

The full recipe referenced (and heavily paraphrased) in this webpage can be found at: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/223234/macaron-french-macaroon/.

Ingredients

  • Egg Whites (3)
  • White Sugar (1/4 cup)
  • Confectioners' Sugar (1 and 2/3 cups)
  • Finely ground Almonds (1 cup)

Steps

  1. With a silicone baking mat, line a baking sheet.
  2. Beat egg whites until foamy. After, add white sugar and continue beating egg whites until they are fluffy, glossy, and hold soft peaks.
  3. Sift ground almonds and confectioners' sugar in a different bowl, then swiftly fold the mixture into the egg whites (roughly 30 strokes).
  4. Spoon a little bit of batter into a plastic bag and cut a small corner off. Test the batter by piping a disk (about 3.8 cm) onto the baking sheet. If the disk does not flatten immediately and instead holds a peak, fold the batter a little more and retry.
  5. Once the batter is able to flatten immediately into a disk, spoon it into a pastry bag with a plain round tip. Leaving space in between disks, pipe the batter onto the baking sheet. Let the disks remain at room temperature until their tops harden (approximately 1 hour).
  6. Preheat oven to 140 degrees Celsius.
  7. Bake disks in oven until set, though not browned (approximately 10 minutes).
  8. Cool disks fully before filling (about half an hour).
