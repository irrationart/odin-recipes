Crepes

Description

The recipe below should not be used as an actual recipe.

The full recipe referenced (and heavily paraphrased) in this webpage can be found at: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/19037/dessert-crepes/.

Ingredients

  • Lightly beaten Eggs (4)
  • All-Purpose Flour (1 cup)
  • White Sugar (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt (1 teaspoon)
  • Melted Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Milk (1 and 1/3 cups)

Steps

  1. Whisk all the ingredients together in a big bowl until it is smooth.
  2. Warm a medium frying or crepe pan over medium heat. With a small amount of oil or butter, grease the pan with a brush or paper towel.
  3. Spoon approximately 3 tablespoons of batter onto the heated pan. Evenly coat the bottom of the pan by tilting it. Cook each side for approximately 1 or 2 minutes until it is golden brown.
  4. Serve when warm.
